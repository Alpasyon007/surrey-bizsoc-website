Who We Are

We’re a diverse group of University of Surrey students who aim to create a collaborative community of students who want to enhance their careers, make friends and understand business better.

We’re not all Business students but we all understand the importance of Business across a range of industries. Our event teams work to bring the some of the highest quality events to students!

President’s Message

Janhvi Sehgal, President 2020/21

“The riskiest thing we can do is just maintain the status quo” - Bob Iger.

"As a commitment to ensure we are providing the best business-related opportunities to our students, we’ve realigned our objectives as a society to ensure we are engaging with students across a multitude of industry- and career-specific topics. Following our restructure to include four divisional areas within Events, as well as an expansion to our Publicity team, we have been working hard all summer to be in a position to bring the most and highest quality events that Surrey Business Society has ever created. Throughout the year, we will be creating opportunities for you to network, engage with companies and discover your potential within the world of business; whilst finding out what you have a true passion for and nurturing your skills within that area."

We want to support our society members to not only overcome the challenges this year continues to bring, but to thrive despite them. The times we find ourselves in are unprecedented to say the least, but we want to make it easier for you to navigate your time at University. As such, we will always ensure our events are safe and welcoming, with unparalleled quality in terms of our speakers and themes; we have successfully held online events over summer which have had high engagement and we can’t wait to provide even more for you in the upcoming academic year.

Aside from giving you the tools to best prepare for your professional career, Surrey Business Society will be holding social events throughout the year to get to know our members better; and provide a platform through which you can meet like-minded individuals and create connections for life.

I hope you’re as excited for this year as I am, as we have a lot to look forward to! Don’t hesitate to contact me personally, at the email address provided on the Committee Members page, if you have any questions or queries.

Yours sincerely,

Janhvi Sehgal
